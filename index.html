Faq

As a tattoo Studio, We often receive inquiries from clients regarding their tattoos, pricing or the process of booking an appointment. To help address these common queries, We have compiled a list of frequently asked questions. If you have any additional inquiries, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.

How do I book an appointment?

If you are interested in a tattoo appointment, just send us an email (please don't send us messages through social media). We will get back to you soon with possible dates.

How long is the waiting period?

Depending on your booking and how many clients have booked. It usually doesn't take that long.

How much will it cost?

Check our pricing or send us an email.

What if I'm not satisfied?

You can send us an email or book an appointment to discuss your situtation.
